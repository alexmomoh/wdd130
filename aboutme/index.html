I am a member of the The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Days Saint for over 25 years. One of my favourite scriptures is Mosiah 2:17 "And behold, I tell you these things that ye may learn wisdom; that ye may learn that when ye are in the service of your fellow beings ye are only in the service of your God."
